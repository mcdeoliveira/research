Text Book

This year we ordered a custom version of our textbook which will be sold at the UCSD bookstore at a signfinicant discount. It should be in stock in time for the class:

The Analysis and Design of Linear Circuits, 6th Edition (UCSD Custom), R. E. Thomas, A. J. Rosa and G. J. Toussaint, Wiley 2009. ISBN 9780470953457

This custom version contains only a selection of chapters from the standard hardcover version of the book:

The Analysis and Design of Linear Circuits, 6th Edition, R. E. Thomas, A. J. Rosa and G. J. Toussaint, Wiley 2009. ISBN 0-470-38330-5

Of course you can buy the hardcover version or you may also choose to buy used earlier editions, such as

The Analysis and Design of Linear Circuits, 5th Edition, R. E. Thomas and A. J. Rosa, Wiley 2006. ISBN 0-471-76095-1

Homework will be assigned from the 6th Edition though. 6th Edition is on the Science and Engineering Library Reserves.

Lecture Notes

Syllabus

Part I

  • Introduction (T & R, Chapters 1 & 2)
  • Equivalent circuits (T & R, Chapters 2 & 3)
  • Systematic circuit analysis (T & R, Chapter 3)
  • Active circuits (T & R, Chapter 4)

Part II

  • Laplace transform (T & R, Chapter 9 & 6)
  • Circuits in the s-domain (T & R, Chapter 6 & 10)
  • S-domain circuit analysis and design (T & R, Chapter 10)
  • Frequency response and filter design (T & R, Chapter 12 & 14)

Elvis Notes

  • Equivalent Circuits
  • Operational Amplifier
  • Summer
  • Filter
