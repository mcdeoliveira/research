Course Topics

  • Analysis of feedback systems
    • Dynamic models
    • Differential equation models and transfer functions
    • Dynamic response
    • Stability, tracking and regulation
  • Design of feedback systems
    • PID control
    • Root-locus method
    • Frequence response
    • Bode and Nyquist diagrams
