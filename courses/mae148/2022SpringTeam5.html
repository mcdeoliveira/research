Connector to Change Batteries

Goal: Change batteries without tedious disassembly

  • Doesn’t require turning off of system
  • Disconnect with a switch
  • Use a diode system
Final Design

  • 2 battery input, 1 battery output
  • 4 switches
    • 2 for battery switching
    • 2 for current flow
  • Switches - 50 A
  • Diodes - 12 A
